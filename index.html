AL-HARAMAIN TOURS

CHINA & HONGKONG

ISLAMIC HISTORICAL TOUR
  • Beijing, Xi'an & Hong Kong
  • Premium 5-Star Halal Dining
  • Historically Significant Mosques
  • Bullet Train Experience Included
  • Expert English-speaking Guides
Spiritual Guide Shaikh Nabil
10-OCT-2026 to 20-OCT-2026
Day 1 • Sunday, 11 October 2026
Arrival - Beijing
  • Welcome at Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK) by our tour representative.
  • VIP airport transfer to your luxury 5-star hotel in the heart of Beijing.
  • Rest and acclimatize after your flight.
  • Orientation welcome dinner at a renowned local Halal roast duck restaurant.
  • Overnight in Beijing.
Day 2 • Monday, 12 October 2026
Beijing Landmarks
  • Early morning tour of the iconic Tiananmen Square and the majestic Forbidden City.
  • Midday prayer at the historic Niujie Mosque, Beijing's oldest mosque dating back to the Liao Dynasty (996 AD).
  • Halal lunch featuring local hand-pulled beef noodles.
  • Visit the Summer Palace, the stunning imperial garden of the Qing Dynasty.
  • Halal hot pot dinner and cultural tea ceremony.
  • Overnight in Beijing.
Day 3 • Tuesday, 13 October 2026
The Great Wall Experience
  • Travel to the Mutianyu section of the Great Wall of China. Cable car ride up and back down.
  • Group photo session on the historic towers.
  • Visit the Cloisonné enamel craft factory to learn traditional art.
  • Visit the Olympic Green, including photo stops at the Bird's Nest and Water Cube.
  • Prayer session at Dongsi Mosque.
  • Halal barbecue dinner.
  • Overnight in Beijing.
Day 4 • Wednesday, 14 October 2026
Beijing to Xi'an
  • Transfer to Beijing West Railway Station to board the high-speed bullet train to Xi'an.
  • Scenic train journey across the Chinese countryside (approx. 4.5 hours).
  • Arrival in Xi'an, the ancient capital and starting point of the Silk Road.
  • Check in to your premium 5-star hotel.
  • Evening walking tour of the lively Muslim Quarter and its vibrant food bazaar.
  • Overnight in Xi'an.
Day 5 • Thursday, 15 October 2026
Xi'an Heritage
  • Guided tour of the world-famous Terracotta Warriors and Horses Museum, an archaeological wonder.
  • Perform Juma'ah / midday prayers at the Great Mosque of Xi'an, founded in 742 AD during the Tang Dynasty.
  • Halal dumpling banquet dinner, a local specialty.
  • Night view of the ancient City Wall and the Giant Wild Goose Pagoda plaza.
  • Overnight in Xi'an.
Day 6 • Friday, 16 October 2026
Xi'an - Shanghai & Beyond
  • Morning flight to Shanghai, China's futuristic commercial hub.
  • Walk along The Bund, enjoying views of the historic colonial buildings and the Pudong skyline.
  • Perform prayers at the Huxi Mosque.
  • Visit the Yu Garden and the Bazaar area.
  • Grand farewell dinner and cruise on the Huangpu River.
  • Overnight in Shanghai.
